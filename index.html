aPHIDS

Aphids: Also known as greenflies or plant lice, these sap-sucking insects are loved by ants, less so by gardeners. Signs & Symptoms Aphids often congregate near stems at the tops of plants. Ants are drawn to aphids, so a gathering of ants is a strong indication. Prevention Avoid cross-contamination: don't use soil or tools from your exterior garden in your hydroponic set-up. Aphids like synthetic fertilizers. Treatment Use insecticidal soap. Water washes aphids away.
